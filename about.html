Vision

YEVE is geared towards the total development of the youth in terms of environmental, social, psychological, cultural and spiritual

Mission

To promote youth awarenes towards environmental and development. To be an advocate on preservations of environment, that will uplift the local economy through tourism and to be socially responsible. Finally, be able to contribute to the development of their family and community.

Goals

Goal #1

To empower youth in the preservation and restoration process of the environment.

Goal #2

Provide nurturing environment that attracts tourism and offers livelihood to the community.

Goal #3

Enhance leadership and equip youth and families with competence, knowledge and skills that will enable them to autonomous and face the challenges of daily living.

Goal #4

Develop strategies which reinforce bridges among existing gap in the community with regards to cultural mentalities for restoring youth and family values.
